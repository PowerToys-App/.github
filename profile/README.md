## 🚀 Overview
PowerToys for Windows is a free suite of utilities from Microsoft designed to make everyday tasks quicker and your desktop more your own. Each tool tackles a specific job — tiling windows into custom zones, opening apps with a keystroke, capturing colors, resizing images, batch-renaming files, and lifting text straight off the screen. One Microsoft PowerToys download brings the whole collection to Windows 11 and Windows 10, and a single settings dashboard lets you switch each utility on or off. It's built for developers, IT pros, and anyone who wants a more efficient, customizable Windows experience.

## ✨ Features
- **FancyZones** — Create custom window layouts and snap apps into place for a tidy, organized screen.
- **PowerToys Run** — Launch apps, open files, and run quick searches from a fast keyboard-driven bar.
- **Text Extractor** — Use built-in text recognition to copy words from images, videos, or any part of your screen.
- **Color Picker** — Instantly identify and copy the color of any pixel in the format you prefer.
- **Image Resizer** — Batch-resize images directly from the File Explorer right-click menu.
- **PowerRename** — Rename many files at once with powerful search-and-replace and pattern options.
- **Keyboard Manager** — Remap individual keys and build custom shortcuts that suit your workflow.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is PowerToys free?**
Yes. PowerToys is a free, open-source project from Microsoft. Every utility is available at no cost, and there's no subscription to pay.

**What is PowerToys Text Extractor?**
PowerToys Text Extractor is a utility that copies text from anywhere on your screen using optical character recognition. Trigger its shortcut, drag over the area you want, and the recognized text lands on your clipboard — handy for images, screenshots, and windows that don't allow selecting text.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
PowerToys works on modern 64-bit versions of Windows, including Windows 10 (version 2004 or later) and Windows 11. Keeping Windows current helps every utility perform reliably.

**Is my data secure?**
PowerToys runs locally on your PC and operates directly on your windows, files, and clipboard without sending them anywhere. You decide which utilities are enabled and what they can access.
